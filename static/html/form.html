ButtonConfigurator3000

This page will help you configure the buttons on your awesome NodeConf EU Badge.

At the moment you can assign a text and a nfc url for each button

Press the button and the text will show on your screen and the nfc will broadcast the url

Fill out the form, click generate code and you will be taken to the Espruino IDE to upload the code
